e-MAGINE


e-MAGINE is an educational blogging/forum software developed by the Computer Science B.Tech Undergraduate students of IIT-Patna under the able guidance of Dr. Samrat Mondal and Dr. Arijit Mondal. e-MAGINE provides a platform for professors to post educational articles and promote discussions among students which will promote quick and easy learning.

The software also contains an Interactive Algorithm Simulator for better comprehension of Sorting and Graph algorithms.
